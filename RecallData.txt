18V-150|2018 Mercedes Benz C43 AMG, C63S AMG, E63S AMG Sedan, and E63S AMG Stationwagon|AMG performance seats - The backrest rail and backrest fitting may not be properly welded on the driver and front passenger seats. The incorrect welding may result in the seats failing in the event of a crash, increasing the risk of injury. MBUSA will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the welds of the backrest frames, replacing the backrests as necessary, free of charge|May 4, 2018
18V-151|2017 E300 4Matic, E300, and E43 AMG vehicles|LED Headlights - These vehicles may have misadjusted headlights, possibly resulting in = the low beam headlights insufficiently illuminating the roadway. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 108, "Lamps, Reflective Devices, and Associated Equipment." An insufficiently illuminated roadway may increase the risk of a crash. MBUSA will notify owners,and dealers will inspect the headlamp adjustment, adjusting it as necessary, free of charge|April 3,2018
